**Good Correlation Finding**:
> "Your data shows that getting 7-8 hours of sleep correlates with improved mood the next day (r=0.75, p<0.01). Consider prioritizing consistent sleep as part of your wellness routine."

> "We notice your mood scores have been consistently low (<3/10) for the past 14 days. While FeelInk helps track patterns, persistent low mood may benefit from professional support. Would you like information on mental health resources?"
